## Service Summary for Agentic Marketing Use

**Service Name (Primary / Brand):**  
Client Intermediation

**Offered By:**  
Main Street Advisors LTD

**Formal Description:**  
Authorized Client Communication Services

### Core Purpose

Client Intermediation is a professional service offered by **Main Street Advisors LTD** that **acts as an authorized intermediary for clients**, communicating with creditors and financial institutions on the client’s behalf to organize information, present financial positions, and coordinate negotiations—**without providing legal advice or legal representation**.

### What the Service DOES

*   Acts as a **designated liaison** between clients and creditors or financial institutions
*   Communicates account details, financial context, and client instructions
*   Requests hardship accommodations, clarifications, and available options
*   Organizes documentation and correspondence
*   Shields clients from direct, repetitive, or stressful communications
*   Adds structure, professionalism, and consistency to negotiations

### What the Service DOES NOT Do

*   Provide legal advice or legal opinions
*   Act as attorneys or law‑firm representatives
*   Interpret legal rights, statutes, contracts, or court documents
*   Initiate, manage, or defend lawsuits
*   Execute binding legal agreements without explicit client direction
*   Replace licensed legal counsel

### Authority Model

*   Operates under **client‑signed Authorization to Communicate (ATC)**
*   Uses **limited, situation‑specific Power of Attorney only when required by an institution**
*   Client retains **full decision‑making authority at all times**

### Ideal Client Profile

*   Low‑ to moderate‑net‑worth individuals
*   Clients experiencing creditor friction, servicing inefficiencies, or negotiation fatigue
*   Individuals seeking professional support **without entering legal representation**

### Value Proposition (Marketing Angle)

*   A professional buffer between clients and creditors
*   Serious, organized communication institutions respect
*   Reduces emotional burden and cognitive overhead for clients
*   Improves outcomes through discipline, documentation, and persistence
*   Preserves legal escalation as an **option**, not a default

### Tone & Positioning Guidelines for Marketing

*   Professional, calm, and authoritative
*   Client‑aligned, not adversarial
*   Avoid legal terminology (e.g., “legal rights,” “claims,” “defenses,” “representation”)
*   Emphasize coordination, structure, and authorized communication
*   Clearly state the non‑legal scope of the service

### One‑Sentence Marketing Definition

> **Client Intermediation, offered by Main Street Advisors LTD, places a professional, authorized intermediary between clients and creditors—so communications are organized, reduced, and handled with confidence.**

### Mandatory Disclaimer (Short Form)

> Client Intermediation is offered by Main Street Advisors LTD and provides authorized communication and coordination services only. It does not provide legal advice or legal representation.
